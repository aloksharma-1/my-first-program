IIMT University

IIMT University, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh has been named among the 36 top universities in India in ARIIA 2021 Ranking by the Ministry of Education. IIMT is also recognized as “Excellent” among the Higher Education Institution for Innovation and Achievement. IIMT offers UG, PG, Doctoral, Polytechnic, and Diploma courses in the disciplines of Management, Engineering, Science, Humanities, Law, Nursing, Education, etc. The total program fee for the MBA course is about INR 1,03,100 whereas the total B. Tech course fees range from INR 74,100 to INR 1,02,100. IIMT admission is mostly based on the candidate's merit in the last qualifying examination. However, M.Pharm admissions depend on GPAT scores. The university now also accepts Pearson Undergraduate Entrance Exams for international candidates.
